How calculations work: Attack strength = Σ(attack × count) × smithy bonus × brewery bonus × alliance bonus. Defence strength = Σ(def_inf × count) × inf_ratio + Σ(def_cav × count) × cav_ratio, where the ratio reflects the attacker's troop composition (slot 0–2 = infantry, slot 3–5 = cavalry). Wall multiplies total defence per level (Romans/Teutons/Gauls/Egyptians ×3.7%/lvl, Huns ×3.1%/lvl). Casualty ratio = √(loser / winner). In Attack mode all casualties die. In Raid mode: dead ≈ 47% of casualties, wounded ≈ 19% (return after raid) — empirically verified against Travian's official simulator.
